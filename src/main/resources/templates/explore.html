Mesmerizing Journy to Deomali Hill

The highest mountain Peak of Odisha Deomali 1762 mt. high nestling in the lap of the Eastern Ghats is an idealic for Aero Gliding and trekking expeditions.

About:

The highest mountain Peak of Odisha Deomali 1762 mt. high nestling in the lap of the Eastern Ghats is an idealic for Aero Gliding and trekking expeditions. It is 60 Kms away from Koraput. Located at a distance of 65 km from Koraput town, Deomali is easily accessible by road. During the last ten odd km, I stopped multiple times and gazed at nature’s beauty with unbridled enthusiasm and happiness. As I kept climbing further and approached the last mile, the views and the feeling of being in the lap of Deomali Koraput were genuinely captivating and astounding. Deomali truly is OUTSTANDING and BREATHTAKING. The majestic views with a range of hills spread out in front of me as I gazed far into the horizon cannot be explained and put into words; such is the magnificence of being in the Eastern Ghats of India. Layer after layer of mountain ranges presented themselves, standing together as a band of brothers and DEOMALI peaking in above all as the charismatic leader.

Facts about Deomali Hill
  1. While Odisha enjoys a mild to moderate temperature, everything changes at Deomali. The temperature at Deomali is always considerably lower than the Koraput temperature or Semiliguda temperature. So be prepared accordingly for Deomali Weather.
  2. Solo travel at night to the Deomali hilltop is to be avoided.
  3. There is a single ghat road, and it is risky at night owing to wild animals and utter loneliness
  4. There is nothing at Deomali in case of any emergency
  5. Deomali mountain peak is in the middle of tribal land, with many tribes inhabiting the mountain villages. So, one needs to be mindful of this fact.
